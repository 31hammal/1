STEAM Learning & How It Works

ThermoBuddy is a great project for mixing Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math. Build one to explore thermochromic pigments, 3D design, and how heat changes materials.

Educational activities

  • Measure the temperature where the color flips and graph your results.
  • Test different sleeve thicknesses ‚Äî which changes color faster?
  • Mix pigments and document the color transitions over time.

Safety & materials

  • Use non-toxic thermochromic pigments meant for crafts or plastics.
  • 3D print the shell in PLA or PETG and apply pigment to a visible panel.
  • All electronic demos are low-voltage and child-friendly when supervised.
